    Ditch Alt-Tabbing

    The Radio System is a powerful and flexible system for Roblox, allowing you to stop alt-tabbing between Roblox and other platforms to switch radio channels.

    Radio System supports both text and voice chat, allowing your players to communicate with each other seamlessly. Additionally, you can finely control who can access the radio system, and what channels.

    Ditch complex Discord channel setups, alt-tabbing and learn what a real radio system is.
